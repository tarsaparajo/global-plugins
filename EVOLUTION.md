DOCTRINE wiring: `skills/generate/SKILL.md` (steps 4 + 7 — seed the skeleton ecosystem, author a bespoke `hero.svg`, convert to PNG) and `skills/adapt/SKILL.md` (step 5 — a hero-less plugin gains one authored from the skeleton; an existing hero is preserved); `skills/_knowledge/readme-skeleton.md` §7 now points to the dedicated doctrine; `agents/plugin-architect.md` + `agents/capability-extractor.md` describe the hero as a shared skeleton; both asset READMEs document the ecosystem + the palette/logo knobs + the conversion.
